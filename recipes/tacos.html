Chicken Tacos

Description

A bonafide Tuesday classic! This is a simple dish of chopped or shredded, lightly seasoned boneless chicken breast topped with shredded mexican cheese, chopped lettuce, your choice of diced tomato or pico de gallo, and sweet & sour sauce. All wrapped in a flour tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Flour Tortillas
  • 1 head of Lettuce
  • 1 Tomato or 1 small container of Pico de Gallo
  • 1/2 lb. bag of Shredded Mexican Cheese
  • 1 bottle of Sweet & Sour Sauce
  • 1 bottle of Garlic Powder
  • 1 bottle of 21 Seasoning

Directions

  1. Pour water into baking tray, enough for water to fill the bottom. Prepare boneless chicken by lightly seasoning each chicken breast with garlic powder and 21 Seasoning. Place each breast into baking tray. Let sit until oven is preheated.
  2. Preheat oven to 275 F. Place chicken in oven once preheated and cook for 1 hour or until chicken starts to brown
  3. Chop chicken to desired size pieces or until "shredded"
  4. Chop lettuce to desired size pieces. Dice tomatoes if using them
  5. Place chopped/shredded chicken onto flour tortilla until desired amount is reached. Top chicken with shredded Mexican cheese, sweet & sour sauce, lettuce, and either tomato or pico de gallo. Enjoy!
